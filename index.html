About

A life spent in beauty's company

Welcome. I'm so glad you're here. I am a Beautifier — it's the closest word I have for what I do — adorning walls, bodies, tables, spaces, moments. In art, I found a soulmate, a lifetimes-old companion. A force that has been with me through the darkest seasons of my life and kept the channel to my inner world open when everything else was noise. Since my first painting, I've cultivated myself in the arts and I'm a bona fide Venusian enthusiast.

Now that you're here, check out how we can work together, poke around in my fashion world, read about my upcoming show ideas, or intellectualize with me about classical art and the world we live in. Before you leave, be sure to peruse my enormous body of work — you're sure to find something you like, and you can reach out about buying a piece.

Beyond the seen, what I'm actually motivated by is how to Be Love. I believe that's what gets humanity to its next point. Inner peace first. Then community. Then a world built by people who let love lead. Join me →
