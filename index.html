About

A life spent
in beauty's company

I wake up every morning and make art before I look at my phone. This is not a productivity hack. It's a practice — a daily recommitment to the inner world before the outer one rushes in. I meditate, I stay off the internet until I'm ready. Then I go looking for beauty, because beauty is what I came here for.

At my core, I am a Beautifier. It's the closest word I have for what I do — adorning walls, bodies, spaces, moments, lives. Art found me in a gap year and never gave me back, and I'm the winner here. In art, I found a soulmate, a lifetimes-old companion. A force that has been with me through the darkest seasons of my life and kept the channel to my inner world open when everything else was noise. I am, above all, a Venusian enthusiast.

What I'm actually obsessed with, underneath all of it, is how to Be Love. I believe that's what gets humanity to its next point. Not technology, not systems, not policy — though I believe in the critical role of all of those too. Inner peace first. Then community. Then a world built by people who let love lead.
