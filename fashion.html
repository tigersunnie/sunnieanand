Fashion & Fabrics

Let's slow it down

In fashion, I sew, I construct, I rework. I work as a tailor, wardrobe and character designer, and a stylist with repeat clients. I've sold over $20k in personal styling, worked boutiques, film sets, and private consults — and I have a talent for the thrift that borders on the supernatural. What can I say? The pieces just find me.

I've taken friends on styling and thrifting trips across Paris, Italy, Mexico, London, and beyond, always with hauls that border on the unforgettable.

If the right producer is reading this, hear me out on a series with serious potential: eight episodes streaming, four continents, and Me, going thrifting. I take the viewer with me into the sophisticated showrooms, the occasional Goodwill, the vintage markets of Paris and Mexico City — all while my fashion fairies whisper in my ear and lead me straight to the good finds. It could be absolutely fabulous. Call me. 😉

We are the only species that creates waste it cannot absorb back into itself. Let's stop acting like sick people and start Being the Creators we are and build a better world. Join me.→
